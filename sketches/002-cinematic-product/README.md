## Variant: Cinematic Product

### Design stance
A dark premium narrative where photography and product evidence create drama without compromising reading flow.

### Key choices
- Layout: standard sticky nav, readable hero, indexed work list, split profile, writing cards
- Typography: compressed modern sans with mono system labels
- Color: near-black, white, one cyan interaction color
- Interaction: switch the profile section between story and operating principle

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: premium feel, tech-forward confidence, image impact
- Weak at: darker and more serious than the other directions

### Best for
A founder/operator identity where the portfolio should feel engineered and current.
